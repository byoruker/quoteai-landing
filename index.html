Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need to know AI to use this?

No. You just describe the job in plain English — "fix kitchen sink, replace P-trap, Chicago IL" — and QuoteAI does the rest. No prompts, no templates, no learning curve.

How accurate are the AI-generated prices?

QuoteAI uses current 2025-2026 US market rates by trade and location. You can upload your own supplier price sheets for even more accuracy. Every line item is editable before sending.

Does my client need to download an app?

No. Your client gets an email with a link. They click it, review the quote in their browser, type their name to accept. That's it. Works on any phone or computer.

Is the digital acceptance legally binding?

Digital acceptance (e-signature) is legally recognized in all 50 US states under the ESIGN Act and UETA. We recommend consulting your attorney for specific legal advice regarding your state and industry.

Can I use my own logo and business name?

Yes. Go to Settings → Business Profile, upload your logo, add your business name, license number, payment terms, and signature. All quotes are branded to your business.

What trades does QuoteAI support?

64 trades across 7 categories: Construction, Mechanical, Outdoor & Property, Home Services, Auto Services, Creative Services, and Specialty Services. Roofing, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, painting, cleaning, pest control, auto detailing, and many more.

Can I edit the quote before sending?

Absolutely. Every line item is fully editable. Change descriptions, quantities, prices, categories. Add charges, discounts, taxes, travel fees. The AI gives you a starting point — you're always in control.

What happens when a client accepts?

You get an instant notification. The quote status changes to "Accepted." You can convert it to an invoice, log job expenses, and track profit/loss all in one place. Everything stays organized automatically.

Is my data secure?

Yes. QuoteAI is built on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security — your data is completely isolated from other users. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or share your data.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime with no penalties. You keep access until the end of your billing period. Your data is available for export before you leave. No lock-in, no tricks.
